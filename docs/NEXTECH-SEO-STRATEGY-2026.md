This strategy positions NexTech Fusion GmbH as the dominant player in AI-powered knowledge management for the German Mittelstand (SME) trades and construction sector. **Key Update (March 2026):** Prioritized keywords into 3 tiers based on validated market research and competitor gap analysis. Focus on low-competition, high-intent keywords first.

## Part 3: AI Search Optimization (GEO/AEO)

### 3.1 Understanding AI Search Engines

**Key Players:**
- Perplexity AI (780M+ queries/month)
- ChatGPT Search (OpenAI)
- Google SGE (Search Generative Experience)
- Claude (Anthropic)
- Microsoft Copilot

**How They Differ from Google:**
1. **Cite authoritative sources** rather than rank pages
2. **Extract passages** rather than whole pages
3. **Prefer structured data** (FAQs, tables, lists)
4. **Value freshness** (last updated dates)
5. **Ignore backlinks** (mostly)

### 3.2 GEO Implementation Strategy

#### A. Content Structure for AI Extraction

```yaml
Optimal Structure:
  Opening: Direct answer in first paragraph (50-100 words)
  Structure: Clear H2/H3 hierarchy
  Format: Bulleted lists, numbered steps, comparison tables
  Data: Statistics, percentages, original research
  
Example Format:
  ---
  ## [Question keyword]?
  
  [Direct answer in 2-3 sentences with key data]
  
  ### Key Points:
  - Point 1 with specific numbers
  - Point 2 with comparison
  - Point 3 with outcome
  
  ### Detailed Explanation:
  [500-800 words of supporting content]
  
  ### Quick Facts Table:
  | Metric | Value |
  |--------|-------|
  | [Data] | [Number] |
  ---
```

#### B. Schema Markup for AI Visibility

```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [
    {
      "@type": "Question",
      "name": "Was ist Wissenssicherung für Handwerksbetriebe?",
      "acceptedAnswer": {
        "@type": "Answer",
        "text": "Wissenssicherung im Handwerk bedeutet, dass das Expertenwissen Ihrer Monteure dauerhaft für das Unternehmen verfügbar bleibt. NexWorker erfasst dieses Wissen automatisch aus WhatsApp-Kommunikationen und macht es durchsuchbar. Statistiken zeigen: 70% des Wissenstransfers findet informell statt."
      }
    }
  ]
}
```

**Required Schema Types:**
1. **FAQPage** - Highest citation rate for AI engines
2. **HowTo** - Step-by-step processes
3. **Article** - Blog posts with author, datePublished, dateModified
4. **SoftwareApplication** - NexWorker product specs
5. **Organization** - NexTech Fusion GmbH entity
6. **BreadcrumbList** - Site navigation
